  • voodooKobra – Encrypt ALL of the Things! for Webmasters

    Bio

    Scott is a web programmer who has been writing PHP and Javascript since he was in middle school. As he does not yet consider himself an expert, Scott holds other hackers, developers, and sysadmins to a fairly high standard. Scott loves to learn and to share what he has already learned.

    Abstract

    Come to the “going dark” side, and learn how to set up a website that adequately protects the privacy of your users.

    Topics covered:

    • Deploying a website with HTTPS
    • Deploying a Tor Hidden Service
    • Publish a Tor Hidden Service behind a dedicated reverse proxy (using Onionimbus, if it’s stable; otherwise, with a simple nginx proxy_pass)
