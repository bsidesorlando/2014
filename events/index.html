Capture the Flag


Miami p0wn Machine is back for another challenging CTF! They will be live during the conference in the Events Hall. Make sure to stop by and get your hack on!

Jax Locksport and Jax TOOOL will be hosting our lockpick village. Take a crack at lock picking, stock up on toools, and try out every hacker’s other favorite hobby!

Come hack live targets though our cooperation with Bugcrowd. Real websites, real bounties, real money. The live action starts Saturday after the talks have ended. Stick around for the exploit fun through the evening and learn about responsible disclosure by example.
